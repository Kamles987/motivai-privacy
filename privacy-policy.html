Privacy Policy for MotivAI

Last updated: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MotivAI ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use the MotivAI mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We do not require account registration and we do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number in the App.

The App may process limited technical information through third-party services for functionality and ads, such as:

  • Device and app identifiers
  • Approximate location (derived from IP, if provided by third-party services)
  • Ad interaction and diagnostic data

2. How the App Uses Data

  • To fetch motivational quotes from third-party quote APIs
  • To display ads using Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)
  • To save app preferences and cached quote content locally on your device
  • To let you share generated quote images through apps you choose

3. Local Storage on Your Device

MotivAI stores certain data locally (for example, cached quotes and app state/preferences) to improve performance and user experience. This data stays on your device unless you share content yourself.

4. Third-Party Services

The App relies on third-party services that may collect or process data under their own policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • ZenQuotes API (quote content)
  • Farzi Vichar API (Hindi quote content)

Please review the privacy policies of these providers for details on their data practices.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by third-party providers only to deliver app features and advertising.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intentionally directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect app functionality and data handling, but no internet or electronic storage method is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via GitHub: https://github.com/Kamles987/motivai/issues
